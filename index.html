Good day to you all, my fine gentlemen and gentlewomen. It is my hope to indulge you with entertainment and popcorn, for what is a good show without a snack?

Last night I had a dream!

In the recesses of my drowsy mind, I was flipping through candid photographs of drunken festivities.

But here's something strange:

In each of these photos was a person who had caught the eye of the camera just as it blinked. It seemed as if they were not part of the moment, but rather had transcended the millisecond restriction of the snapshot.
